About Algorithmic Bias

Algorithmic bias occurs when AI systems make decisions that systematically disadvantage certain groups of people. This scenario explores how AI can perpetuate existing social inequalities.

Real-world Example: Amazon's AI Recruiting Tool

In 2018, Amazon had to scrap an AI recruiting tool that showed bias against women. The system was trained on resumes submitted over a 10-year period, which were mostly from men, leading it to favor male candidates.

Digital Footprint Impact

Your digital footprint - including your online activities, social media presence, and digital interactions - can significantly influence how AI systems perceive and categorize you, affecting opportunities in education, employment, and more.
